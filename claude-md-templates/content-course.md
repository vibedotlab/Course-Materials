## Voice and Style
- Audience: Complete beginners — no assumed knowledge
- Tone: Direct, warm, slightly informal
- Every topic: Explanation → Real-world Analogy → Plug-and-play Example
- No filler phrases ("It's important to note that...")
- Target: 800-1500 words per chapter

## Rules
- Every example must work without modification
- Every paid feature must show the cost before the reader encounters it
- No jargon without inline definition on first use
- Define all acronyms on first use
